Previously employed by SANSA (South African National Space Agency) as an Electronic Engineer in a remote sub-Antarctic island called Marion Island.

Currently pursuing a MEng in Electronic/Electrical Engineering from the Cape Peninsula University of Technology, Cape Town and my Bachelor of Electronic Engineering from the Tshwane University of Technology, Pretoria.
